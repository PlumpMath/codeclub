Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?

This game is designed to show you how to learn by yourself. You can do it. Keep going even when the going gets tough.

All questions are optional and there are no right or wrong answers. Doing this list with some friends or a parent might be more fun!

Do them in any order you like and share and improve your answers with friends, this is *not* a test! You can each do different questions and share at the end if you like.

Be sure to write some questions, thoughts and keyword notes down for yourself and keep it safe, that is how you get smarter.

Epilogue: The Hive Mind Strikes Back

Sometimes what makes some people the best at what they do is their perseverance even when things are really tough. Even if you don't understand a word of the question, still giving it a go and not beating yourself up if you don't get it right is a massive achievement. The best way to learn is to teach yourself, because that way there's no limit to what you can learn! If you're really up for a challenge, try a free GMAT test. These are made for people going to business school, some of the Tech Super Heroes have been here before you and they leave you a message that gives you hope on your way. If you get one question right that's a really good job! Go to Problem Solving. Check the explanation after you're done, even if you got it right - and be careful young adventurers, the force is strong within you, but these ones, they are designed to be tricky!
